WHAT IS PAWFECT ?

Pawfect is a platform built to make pet owners & our partners' life easier :

- Pet owners : All the different kinds of services booked in one click gathered in a one unique platform. Compare the price, the location, the reviews, all the tools to make the best choice.

- Partners : All your bookings automatically updated in our platform. Implement your current bookings and personalize your calendar by showing your availabilities. Pawfect is here to help you focus on your core work.
